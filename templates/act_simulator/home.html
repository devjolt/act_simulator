Welcome

This free resource generates infinite Army Cognitive Test practise questions based on the official version here.

Features:

  • unlimited randomised questions (practice ad-nauseum without question repetition)
  • measure speed* (average time per correct response)
  • track accuracy* (percentage responses correct)
  • timer displayed during your session
  • unlimited practise time
  • unlimited sessions in each sub-test

* Whilst final test scores aren't provided by this resource, speed and accuracy are used to calculate your score on the real ACT

DISCLAIMER: The question archetypes in each sub-section are based on the official ACT practice resource and only reflect the actual test to the extent that the official practice version does (although I, and those I know of who've used this resource to prepare for the ACT, thought it was spot on!)
