This free resource generates Army Cognitive Test practise questions based on the official version here (opens in a new tab).
Features:

  • unlimited randomised questions (practice ad-nauseum without question repetition)
  • measure speed* (average time per correct response)
  • track accuracy* (percentage responses correct)
  • timer displayed during your session
  • unlimited practise time
  • unlimited sessions in each sub-test

* Whilst test scores aren't provided by this resource, speed and accuracy are used to calculate your score on the real ACT

DISCLAIMER: The question archetypes in each sub-section are based on the official ACT practice resource. They only reflect the actual test to the extent that the official version does.
